Cards

A card is a flexible and extensible content container. It includes options for headers and footers, a wide variety of content, contextual background colors, and powerful display options.

Example of vertical cards

Vertical cards are available in 3 types- Basic card, Basic card with header, Blog card.
To use this card just use class basic-card along with classes to style the card like card-body, card-footer, card-btn, card-title, card-content.

card-title: For card title
card-subtext: For card title subtext
card-content: For card content
card-icon: For card footer icons
header-text: For card with header's header section.
