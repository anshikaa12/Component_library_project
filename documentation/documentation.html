card-title: For card title
card-subtext: For card title subtext
card-content: For card content
card-icon: For card footer icons

Modals

Modals are built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They’re positioned over everything else in the document and remove scroll from the body so that modal content scrolls instead.

Example of standard Modal

Modals comes in 2 variation: Static Modal, click-anywhere Modal.
The static Modal only closes by clicking on the close button whereas the click-anywhere Modal closes by clicking anywhere on the screen.

modal : modal styling
click-anywhere-modal : Parent div ID for click anywhere modal.
static-modal : Parent div ID for static modal.
close-modal : to close the modal.

Snackbars are available in three positions: leading, baseline, stacked

leading-bar: To position the snackbar at bottom left.
baseline-bar: To position the snackbar at bottom mid.
stacked-bar: To position the bar at the bottom right with stacked structure.
snack-close: For the close icon on snackbar.
